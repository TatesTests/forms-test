    • EXTENSION CHALLENGE

    BUILD EXTENSIONS THAT FUEL THE TWITCH COMMUNITY

    Build an Extension to win up to $15,000

    AWS and Twitch are working together to launch the Twitch AWS Extension Challenge. By building Twitch Extensions and integrating with AWS, one is eligible to enter the challenge. The prizes for winning include cash awards up to $15,000, tickets to TwitchCon 2019, and even meetings with Twitch leadership to talk through ideas.

    Sign Up to Learn More

    Challenge Details

    Build a Twitch Extension for viewers, broadcasters, or both that integrates or is built with at least one AWS service.

    Extension must be submitted by Tuesday, February 26.

    We are awarding over $25,000 in prize money, with the winner getting $15,000 and a chance to meet with Twitch leadership to discuss their Extension.

    Seven winners determined by judges and one “crowd favorite” will receive prizes.

     

    Why Develop Extensions on Twitch?

    Twitch has a large viewership and they are highly engaged (15 million daily active users and 355 billion minutes watched in 2017). This means there is a high potential to get a lot of visibility building on Twitch.

    With millions of people gathering on Twitch every day, developers have the opportunity to create new ways to consume and contribute to live programming with Twitch Extensions.

    There is lots of enthusiasm for Extensions from our community of viewers and chatters. Since launch, just over a year ago, people have interacted with Extensions over 4.9 BILLION times.

    There’s a full set of developer tools for Extensions, enabling developers to build these immersive experiences easily and quickly.

    There’s a direct opportunity for developers to monetize on Twitch. Bits-in-Extensions enables viewers to show support to the streamer, enhance gameplay, or express themselves. Streamers and developers share the revenue generated from these Bits.
